📋 Enrollment Windows Quick Reference
ACA OEP
November 1 – January 15
Annual Open Enrollment Period. Anyone can enroll, switch, or change ACA health plans. Primary campaign months: November (launch), December, January (close).
Medicare AEP
October 15 – December 7
Annual Enrollment Period. Anyone on Medicare can switch or enroll in Advantage or PDP plans. Highest urgency window of the year. Dec 7 is a hard close.
Medicare OEEP
January 1 – March 31
Open Enrollment for Existing Enrolled Period. Those already on Medicare Advantage can switch once. January is a fresh opportunity; March 31 is the close.
SEP
Ongoing · Triggered by Life Events
Special Enrollment Period. Triggers: job loss, marriage, birth, moving, aging off parent plan at 26, income change. Always available — key for ACA year-round targeting.
