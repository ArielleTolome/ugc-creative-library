💡 Creative Tips
Tip 01
Match accent to location. A Texas accent in a NYC background reads wrong. Regional coherence builds trust subconsciously.
Tip 02
Occupation drives wardrobe. Don't mix. A nurse in a tool belt breaks immersion immediately — don't make viewers think.
Tip 03
Energy level should match format. Raw UGC: 75%. DR Hook: 90–100%. Ambient Slice: 45–60%. Mismatched energy reads as fake.
Tip 04
Age targeting by vertical. FE and Medicare prefer 55–75. ACA and Auto are wider. Use age range to hit the right audience lens.
Tip 05
Hook type by format. Confession hooks work best for Raw UGC. Number/stat hooks are made for DR Hook. Match the pattern.
Tip 06
Morning locations feel authentic. Kitchen, porch, car — they signal spontaneous real-talk. Evening couch = reflective. Hospital = credibility.
Tip 07
Professional locations need matching wardrobe. Hospital hallway requires scrubs. Mismatch kills credibility and looks generated.
Tip 08
The random generator creates weird combos. That's intentional. Unexpected combinations surface angles you'd never think to try manually.
